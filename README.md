## ‚ö†Ô∏è Key Business Problems Identified

Through structured analysis and visualization, the following **primary churn drivers** were identified:

1. **Rising customer churn due to poor packaging quality**
2. **Higher delivery times compared to competitors**
3. **Product damage caused by inadequate packaging standards**
4. **Inefficient and slow customer support**
5. **Frequent out-of-stock incidents**
6. **Lack of operational visibility across teams**

Individually minor, collectively catastrophic.

---

## üìä Insights from Explanatory Analysis

* Customers receiving damaged products showed **significantly lower retention**
* Delivery delays had a **direct correlation with negative reviews**
* Stockouts increased cart abandonment and reduced trust
* Support inefficiency amplified dissatisfaction instead of resolving it

üìå **Core Insight:**
QuickBazaar was not losing customers due to pricing or product range, but due to **operational execution failures**.
